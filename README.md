# Financial & Production Performance Analysis 📊

*Author*: Muhammad Amirul Haziq Bin Zahid

## 📝 Project Overview
This repository contains two interactive Power BI dashboards designed to analyze and monitor business performance from two critical angles: *Manufacturing Production* and *Financial Health*. The objective is to identify operational bottlenecks, evaluate cost inefficiencies, and support data-driven decision-making.

## 1️⃣ Production Performance Dashboard
Focused on analyzing machine output, downtime, and operational efficiency.

### 🔑 Key Insights:
- *Efficiency Gap*: Machine M01 operates at 0.95 efficiency, significantly outperforming Machine M02 (0.83).
- *Downtime Bottleneck*: Breakdown maintenance contributes the highest share of total downtime (662 minutes), indicating a reactive rather than preventive maintenance culture.
- *Quality Control*: Machine M02 is responsible for 67.41% of the total defect rate.

### 💡 Business Recommendations:
- Increase preventive maintenance activities, specifically targeting Machine M02, to reduce sudden breakdowns.
- Optimize machine allocation across shifts to balance daily productivity.

---

## 2️⃣ Financial Performance Dashboard
Focused on evaluating revenue growth, departmental profit margins, and cost trends over a multi-year period.

### 🔑 Key Insights:
- *Revenue Growth*: The company shows consistent year-over-year revenue growth, yet experienced a recent YoY growth decline of -5.16% in 2025.
- *Profit Margin Volatility*: Despite rising revenue, the profit margin fluctuates (e.g., Finance department showing lower margins), indicating escalating operational costs.
- *Regional Performance*: The Northern region is the highest contributor to total profit.

### 💡 Business Recommendations:
- Implement stricter cost-control measures to stabilize profit margins despite rising revenue.
- Diversify revenue streams to reduce dependency on top-performing segments.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- *BI Tool*: Power BI
- *Data Processing*: Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- *Calculations*: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for YoY, YTD, and custom metrics.
