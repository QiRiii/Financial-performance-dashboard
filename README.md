# 📊 Financial Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a financial performance dashboard analyzing revenue, profit, cost, and regional trends. The objective is to identify key business issues and provide data-driven recommendations to improve profitability.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Strong revenue (RM12M) but low profit (RM839K), with ~7% margin indicating margin compression  
- Operational costs increased by approximately 18%, acting as the primary constraint on profitability  
- Marketing spend shows weak ROI, with limited impact on revenue growth  
- Revenue is highly concentrated, with ~60% contributed by top-performing regions  
- Monthly fluctuations (~12%) suggest seasonal demand affecting performance stability  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Optimize operational costs (target reduction: 10–15%) through process improvements  
- Reallocate marketing budget to high-performing channels to improve ROI  
- Focus on top-performing regions while improving underperforming markets  
- Implement demand forecasting to reduce seasonal volatility  
- Align KPIs with profitability (margin, cost ratio, ROI)  

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaway
The business is experiencing margin compression, where rising costs are outpacing revenue growth. A shift toward a profit-first strategy is required to ensure sustainable performance.
